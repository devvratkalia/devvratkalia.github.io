Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

Welcome to AlarmX. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

AlarmX is a sleep wellness application designed to help users wake up effectively through interactive missions. Our primary goal is to provide a functional and reliable alarm experience while respecting user privacy. Most data generated by the app is stored locally on your device.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Device Identifiers: We use third-party services like Google AdMob, which may collect your device's advertising ID and IP address to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Sensor and Camera Data: Our missions (e.g., Barcode Scanning, Pose/Squat Detection) require access to your camera. This processing happens entirely on your device. Captured images or video streams are used only for mission verification and are not transmitted to our servers or stored permanently.
  • App Performance Data: We may collect anonymized crash reports and performance metrics to improve the application.

3. Third-Party Services

AlarmX uses the following third-party services that may collect data used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google ML Kit

4. Data Retention Policy

We believe in data minimization. Here is how we retain your data:

  • Local Data: All your alarm settings, mission history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using an internal database (Room). This data persists as long as the app is installed.
  • Advertising Data: Data collected by AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies.
  • Temporary Data: Camera frames used during missions are processed in real-time in the device's memory and are discarded immediately after the mission ends.

5. Data Deletion and Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Local Data Deletion: You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the App Cache and Data in your Android system settings, or by uninstalling the application.
  • Advertising ID Reset: You can reset or delete your advertising identifier through your Android device's Google Settings (Privacy > Ads).
  • Requesting Deletion: Since we do not maintain user accounts on our own servers, we do not have a centralized database of your personal identity. However, if you have any concerns, you can contact us at the email provided below.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. We use industry-standard security measures to protect the local database on your device and ensure that sensitive permissions (like Camera) are only used when explicitly required for the app's functionality.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

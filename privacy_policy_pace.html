Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Pace, a productivity application developed by Dev_K. Your privacy is paramount to us. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

Pace is designed with a "Local First" philosophy. Most of your productivity data remains exclusively on your device:

  • Tasks & Missions: All task titles, descriptions, and priorities are stored in a local SQLite database (Room). We do not sync this data to any external servers.
  • Vitality Data: Information regarding your physical movement and activity levels is processed locally to calculate your Vitality Score.
  • Oracle Insights: AI-driven productivity predictions are computed entirely on your device using local algorithms.

2. Permissions

To provide advanced productivity features, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Activity Recognition: Used to monitor sedentary behavior and calculate your Vitality and Capacity scores.
  • Notifications: Used to send urgent task reminders and sedentary alerts.
  • Internet Access: Required for Google Play Billing and AdMob advertisement delivery.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for core app functionality and location-based triggers.
  • AdMob: Used to serve advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.
  • Google Play Billing: Used for processing Pace Pro subscriptions. Financial transactions are handled securely by Google; we do not store your credit card information.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your local data. However, as the majority of data is stored on your device, the security of your data also depends on your device's security settings (e.g., screen locks, encryption).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at wekstuff@gmail.com.
